About TXP

In the world full of infinite possibilities and opportunities, there lies a whole lot of innovators, tech enthusiasts or geeks who are just waiting for a chance to do something magical to show their talent. With the aim that technology reaches every hand, the IT and CS department of S.K. Somaiya kick starts its first tech fest extending invitation to all colleges for participation.

Why TechXposure?

It aims in providing a platform to people to showcase their innovative projects, whilst competing to be the best among all other colleges, infront of various startups and potential investors. Giving an exposure to the various aspects of technology, meet and greet with various renowned speakers, indulging into various tech based competitions and hereby knowing their true potential is what we look forward to.
